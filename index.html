The Story of Ocho

Built by Two Friends, Open to Everyone

Ocho began in August 2025, when two friends turned a long-held dream into a place they wanted to exist in their own neighborhood. The idea was simple: create a coffee house that feels warm, open, and easy to spend time in.

We chose the name Ocho because 8, turned on its side, becomes infinity. For us, it reflects the feeling we wanted the space to have: where time slows down, conversations flow, and people feel at home.
